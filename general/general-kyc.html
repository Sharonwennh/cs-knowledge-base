1. Overview / 概述

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is mandatory for all players before they can redeem real prizes. The process is handled through our verification partner Sumsub.
KYC（了解您的客户）验证是所有玩家提现真实奖励前必须完成的步骤。验证流程由我们的合作伙伴 Sumsub 处理。

The standard KYC process consists of two stages: KYC-ID (Identity Verification) and KYC-POA (Proof of Address). In some cases, SSN Verification may be offered as an alternative to POA.
标准 KYC 流程包含两个阶段：KYC-ID（身份验证）和 KYC-POA（地址证明）。在某些情况下，SSN 验证可作为 POA 的替代方式。

Key Point / 要点： KYC review typically takes 1-3 business days. / KYC 审核通常需要 1-3 个工作日。

2. KYC Verification Flow / KYC 验证流程

Step 1: KYC-ID (Identity Verification) / 身份验证

  • Player submits a valid US government-issued ID document. 玩家提交有效的美国政府签发的身份证件。
  • Accepted documents: Driver's License, Passport, State ID Card. 接受的证件：驾照、护照、州身份证。
  • Non-US documents are NOT accepted. 不接受非美国证件。

Step 2: KYC-POA (Proof of Address) / 地址证明

  • Triggered after KYC-ID is approved. 在 KYC-ID 通过审核后触发。
  • Player uploads a proof of address document (utility bill, bank statement, etc.). 玩家上传地址证明文件（水电账单、银行对账单等）。
  • Alternatively, player may be offered SSN Verification as a substitute for POA. 或者，玩家可能会被提供 SSN 验证 作为 POA 的替代。

3. Document Requirements / 证件要求

Identity Document (KYC-ID) / 身份证件

  • Must be a US government-issued photo ID. 必须是美国政府签发的带照片证件。
  • Document must be valid and not expired. 证件必须有效且未过期。
  • Photo must be clear and legible — all text, photo, and security features visible. 照片必须清晰可读 — 所有文字、照片和安全特征可见。
  • No screenshots or photocopies; must be a photo of the physical document. 不接受截图或复印件；必须是实体证件的照片。

Address Document (KYC-POA) / 地址证明文件

  • Must show the same name as the identity document. 必须显示与身份证件相同的姓名。
  • Must be dated within the last 3 months. 日期必须在最近 3 个月内。
  • Accepted: Utility bill, bank statement, government letter, rental agreement. 接受：水电账单、银行对账单、政府信函、租房合同。
  • Not accepted: Mobile phone bills, insurance cards, handwritten documents. 不接受：手机账单、保险卡、手写文件。

4. Enhanced KYC / 高级验证

Enhanced KYC is triggered for high-risk accounts or large redemption requests.
高级验证针对高风险账户或大额提现请求触发。

Critical Rule / 关键规则：
For Enhanced KYC, the identity document and address document must be DIFFERENT documents. You cannot use the same document for both.
高级验证要求身份文件和地址文件必须是不同的证件。不能对两者使用同一份文件。
  • Example: Driver's License for ID + Bank Statement for POA = Valid. 示例：驾照作为 ID + 银行对账单作为 POA = 有效。
  • Example: Driver's License for ID + Driver's License for POA = Invalid. 示例：驾照作为 ID + 驾照作为 POA = 无效。

5. SSN Verification / SSN 验证

SSN verification has the same validity as POA. It is offered as an alternative method when KYC-POA is triggered.
SSN 验证的效力等同于 POA。当 KYC-POA 触发时，可作为替代方式提供。

Verification Levels / 验证等级

Level Name Type / 类型 Required Fields / 必填字段
xxx-ssn-level Full SSN Verification / 完整 SSN 验证 SSN (123-45-6789) + Country
xxx-pssn-level Partial SSN + Phone / 后四位 SSN + 手机验证 Date of birth + Country + Last 4 Digits of SSN + Phone verification code

Flow / 流程

  1. Player completes KYC-ID first. 玩家先完成 KYC-ID。
  2. When KYC-POA is triggered, the player may be directed to SSN verification instead of traditional POA document upload. 当触发 KYC-POA 时，玩家可被引导至 SSN 验证，替代传统 POA 文件上传。
  3. Player selects United States of America as country and enters the required SSN information. 玩家选择 美国 并输入所需 SSN 信息。
  4. For xxx-pssn-level, after entering the last 4 digits of SSN, the player must complete phone verification by entering a valid US phone number to receive a verification code. 对于 xxx-pssn-level，输入 SSN 后四位后，必须输入有效的美国手机号接收验证码完成手机验证。

Rejection Handling / 拒绝处理

If a player's SSN verification is finally rejected, two options are available:
若玩家 SSN 验证被最终拒绝，有两种处理方式：

  • Switch to POA — allow the player to upload traditional Proof of Address documents instead. 切换到 POA — 允许玩家改传传统地址证明文件。
  • Contact Customer Service — if the player is unable to proceed or has questions. 联系客服 — 若玩家无法继续或有疑问。
Privacy Warning / 隐私警告：
CS agents must NEVER ask players to provide their full SSN via chat or email. All SSN data must be entered by the player directly in the Sumsub verification page.
客服绝不能在聊天或邮件中要求玩家提供完整 SSN。所有 SSN 数据必须由玩家直接在 Sumsub 验证页面输入。

6. Common Rejection Reasons / 常见拒绝原因

Reason / 原因Solution / 解决方案
Photo unclear / 照片不清晰 Retake with good lighting, no glare. / 在光线充足、无反光的环境下重新拍摄。
Document expired / 证件过期 Submit a valid, unexpired document. / 提交有效、未过期的证件。
Info mismatch / 信息不匹配 Ensure name matches exactly across all documents. / 确保证件上的姓名完全一致。
Wrong document type / 证件类型错误 Use accepted US ID for KYC-ID; valid POA for KYC-POA. / KYC-ID 使用接受的美国证件；KYC-POA 使用有效地址证明。
Same doc for Enhanced KYC / 高级验证使用同一文件 Provide two different documents. / 提供两份不同的文件。
SSN verification failed / SSN 验证失败 Switch to POA document upload or contact CS. / 切换到 POA 文件上传或联系客服。

7. CS Guidelines / 客服规范

Do's / 应该做

  • Guide players to the Sumsub verification page for all document submissions. 引导玩家前往 Sumsub 验证页面提交所有文件。
  • Explain the 1-3 business day review timeline clearly. 清楚说明 1-3 个工作日的审核时间。
  • For SSN issues, offer to switch to POA if SSN verification fails. SSN 验证失败时，主动提供切换到 POA 的选项。
  • Verify player identity before discussing any KYC details. 在讨论任何 KYC 详情前，先验证玩家身份。

Don'ts / 不应该做

  • Do NOT ask players to send documents via chat/email. 不要要求玩家通过聊天/邮件发送文件。
  • Do NOT ask for full SSN, bank account numbers, or routing numbers. 不要索要完整 SSN、银行账号或路由号码。
  • Do NOT promise exact approval times. 不要承诺确切的通过时间。
  • Do NOT manually approve or bypass KYC for any player. 不要手动批准或绕过任何玩家的 KYC。

8. FAQ / 常见问题

Q: Why do I need to complete KYC? / 为什么我需要完成 KYC？

KYC is required by law to verify your identity before you can redeem real prizes. It helps ensure the security of your account and prevents fraud.

KYC 是法律要求的，在您提现真实奖励前必须验证身份。这有助于确保账户安全并防止欺诈。

Q: I don't have a US ID. Can I use my passport from another country? / 我没有美国证件，可以用其他国家的护照吗？

No. We only accept US government-issued IDs. Non-US documents are not accepted for KYC verification.

不可以。我们只接受美国政府签发的证件。非美国证件不接受用于 KYC 验证。

Q: Why am I being asked for my SSN? / 为什么要求我提供 SSN？

SSN verification is used as a secure alternative to Proof of Address (POA) to confirm your identity. It is processed securely by our verification partner Sumsub and has the same validity as POA.

SSN 验证是作为地址证明（POA）的安全替代方式，用于确认您的身份。由我们的验证合作伙伴 Sumsub 安全处理，效力等同于 POA。

Q: My SSN verification failed. What should I do? / 我的 SSN 验证失败了，怎么办？

If your SSN verification is finally rejected, we can switch you to the standard POA document upload instead. Please upload a valid proof of address document (utility bill, bank statement, etc.).

如果您的 SSN 验证最终被拒绝，我们可以为您切换到标准的 POA 文件上传方式。请上传有效的地址证明文件（水电账单、银行对账单等）。

Q: I don't have a US phone number for pssn-level verification. / 我没有美国手机号来完成 pssn-level 验证。

The phone verification step requires a valid US phone number. If you cannot complete this, please contact us and we will switch you to the POA document upload method or the full SSN verification method instead.

手机验证步骤需要有效的美国手机号。如果您无法完成，请联系我们，我们将为您切换到 POA 文件上传方式或完整 SSN 验证方式。

Q: How long does KYC take? / KYC 需要多久？

KYC review typically takes 1-3 business days. You will be notified once the review is complete.

KYC 审核通常需要 1-3 个工作日。审核完成后您会收到通知。

Q: My KYC was rejected but I don't know why. / 我的 KYC 被拒绝了，但不知道原因。

Please check the rejection reason shown on your verification page. Common reasons include unclear photos, expired documents, or information mismatch. If the reason is unclear, please send us a screenshot and we will assist you.

请查看验证页面上显示的拒绝原因。常见原因包括照片不清晰、证件过期或信息不匹配。如果原因不清楚，请发送截图给我们，我们会协助您。
