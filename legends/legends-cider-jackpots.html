1. Activity Overview / 活动概述

Cider Jackpots is a cumulative jackpot activity with three tiers: Hourly Jackpot, Daily Jackpot, and Cider Jackpot. Every spin gives you a chance to win over 500,000 SC, and all jackpot prizes are awarded in the form of Sweeps Coins (SC).

Cider Jackpots 是一个累积奖池活动，包含三个等级：Hourly Jackpot（每小时奖池）、Daily Jackpot（每日奖池）和 Cider Jackpot（大奖池）。每次旋转都有机会赢得超过 500,000 SC，所有奖池奖品均以 Sweeps Coins (SC) 形式发放。

2. Participation Method / 参与方式

Players need to click the "ENTER CIDER" button in the game to join the activity; click "CIDER OUT" to exit.

玩家需要点击游戏中的"ENTER CIDER"按钮加入活动；点击"CIDER OUT"退出。

After exiting, Players will not be able to participate in subsequent jackpot draws, and the jackpot contribution already paid will not be refunded.

退出后，玩家将无法参与后续的奖池抽奖，且已支付的奖池贡献将不予退还。

After clicking ENTER CIDER, Players can participate in the activity every time you enter a machine with the Cider Jackpots activity icon in the upper left corner.

点击 ENTER CIDER 后，玩家每次进入左上角带有 Cider Jackpots 活动图标的老虎机时都可以参与活动。

The activity only supports participation in the SC game mode, and Players need to play in the designated games marked "Cider Jackpots" using SC mode.

该活动仅支持SC 游戏模式参与，玩家需要在标有"Cider Jackpots"的指定游戏中使用 SC 模式游玩。

3. Jackpot Contribution Rules / 奖池贡献规则

For each eligible SC spin completed by the player, in addition to the normal game place Amount per spin, the system will automatically deduct 0.1 SC from the player's SC balance as the jackpot contribution (regardless of the place Amount per spin for this spin, a fixed 0.1 SC will be deducted).

对于玩家完成的每次符合条件的 SC 旋转，除了正常的每旋转下注金额外，系统会自动从玩家的 SC 余额中扣除0.1 SC作为奖池贡献（无论该次旋转的每旋转下注金额是多少，固定扣除 0.1 SC）。

Jackpot Tier / 奖池等级Distribution / 分配比例
Hourly Jackpot / 每小时奖池27.5%

The jackpot amount will increase in real time with players' contributions, but there may be a slight delay in display; when the player's SC balance is sufficient, the contribution will be deducted automatically for each spin.

奖池金额会随着玩家的贡献实时增加，但显示可能略有延迟；当玩家的 SC 余额充足时，每次旋转会自动扣除贡献。

5. Winning & Prize Distribution / 中奖与奖励发放

  • The jackpot will be randomly triggered during the game spin. When triggered, the corresponding jackpot winning animation will be displayed, which is only valid for the current spin and cannot be claimed repeatedly.
  • 奖池将在游戏旋转过程中随机触发。触发时，会显示对应的奖池中奖动画，仅对当前旋转有效，不可重复领取。
  • After winning, the prize will be automatically added to the player's Unplayed SC balance, and the winning amount must comply with the same redemption restriction rules as other SC prizes.
  • 中奖后，奖品将自动添加到玩家的未游玩 SC余额中，中奖金额须遵守与其他 SC 奖品相同的提现限制规则。
  • Only Played SC can be redeemed; Unplayed SC cannot be redeemed.
  • 只有已游玩 SC 可以提现；未游玩 SC 不可提现。

6. Special Rules / 特殊规则

  • If two players trigger the same jackpot tier to win almost at the same time, the player who triggers it later will receive a new jackpot prize (the amount may be lower than the amount displayed on the page).
  • 如果两名玩家几乎同时触发同一奖池等级中奖，后触发的玩家将获得新的奖池奖品（金额可能低于页面显示的金额）。
  • By participating in the activity, players agree that if they win, their encrypted ID will be displayed in the recent winning list.
  • 参与活动时，玩家同意如果中奖，其加密 ID 将显示在最近中奖列表中。
  • The winning prompt popped up on the page is for reference only; the final winning amount will be credited to the player's balance only after verification and confirmation by the platform.
  • 页面上弹出的中奖提示仅供参考；最终中奖金额仅在平台验证确认后才会计入玩家余额。
  • If the communication between Cider Casino and the jackpot server is interrupted, the jackpot will be temporarily unavailable. During this period, there will be no new contribution or winning operations, the jackpot amount will remain unchanged, and it will resume normal operation after the communication is restored.
  • 如果 Cider Casino 与奖池服务器之间的通信中断，奖池将暂时不可用。在此期间，不会有新的贡献或中奖操作，奖池金额将保持不变，通信恢复后将恢复正常运行。
