📉 Tier Degradation Rules / 等级降级规则

4.1 Gem Expiration Rules (Expires in 90 days) / Gems 过期规则（90 天过期）

Expiration is calculated by calendar days, not by the exact hour or minute, and the first day does not count.

过期按日历日计算，不按精确的小时或分钟计算，且第一天不计入。

Example: Gems obtained anytime on January 1 begin counting from January 2 as Day 1. After 90 full calendar days, the Gems expire at 00:00 on Day 91, regardless of the exact time they were earned.

示例：1 月 1 日任何时间获得的 Gems 从 1 月 2 日开始计为第 1 天。经过 90 个完整日历日后，Gems 在第 91 天的 00:00 过期，无论它们获得的具体时间。

4.2 Tier Downgrade Rules / 等级降级规则

Downgrade is based solely on gem amount. As the gem amount decreases, players' tier will automatically drop to the level that corresponds to their current Gem amount.

降级仅基于 Gems 数量。随着 Gems 数量减少，玩家的等级将自动降至与其当前 Gems 数量对应的等级。

4.3 Downgrade Reminder (Inbox message) / 降级提醒（站内信）

Triggered when: Player is 3 days away from losing current tier

触发条件：玩家距离失去当前等级还有 3 天

FAQ 1: "Can I claim missed daily rewards?" / "我可以领取错过的每日奖励吗？"

Explain: No. Daily rewards expire after 24 hours.

Explain: Store bonus is tier-based, so different players receive different bonus amounts based on their tiers.

解释：商店奖励是基于等级的，因此不同玩家根据等级获得不同的奖励金额。

FAQ 3: "Why doesn't my Thursday awarded feature activate?" / "为什么我的周四 awarded feature 没有激活？"

Explain: Awarded features only activate when entering through the "Thursday Awarded Features" inbox message. It will not be activated when entering through the lobby.

解释：只有通过"周四 Awarded Features"站内信进入时，awarded feature 才会激活。通过大厅进入不会激活。

FAQ 4: "Why am I downgraded? Where did my Gems go?" / "为什么我被降级了？我的 Gems 去哪了？"

Explain: Gems have a 90-day rolling validity. Once expired, they are removed from the account, which may trigger tier downgrade.

解释：Gems 有 90 天滚动有效期。一旦过期，它们将从账户中移除，这可能触发等级降级。

FAQ 5: "Why did my tier suddenly drop to Silver?" / "为什么我的等级突然降到 Silver 了？"

Explain: Your tier is always determined in real time based on your current number of Gems. If you collected a large number of Gems on a certain day 90 days ago, when those Gems all expired on the same day, your total Gem amount dropped, which caused your tier to fall to a lower level.

解释：您的等级始终根据您当前的 Gems 数量实时确定。如果您在 90 天前的某一天收集了大量 Gems，当这些 Gems 在同一天全部过期时，您的总 Gems 数量下降，导致您的等级降至较低级别。
