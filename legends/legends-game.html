1. Day Counter: The day counter advances to the next day only after the player claims the reward. For example: After claiming the Day 1 reward, if the player does not log in for X days, they can still claim the Day 2 reward upon their next login.

1. 天数计数：只有在玩家领取奖励后，天数才会跳转到下一天。例如：领取第 1 天奖励后，如果玩家连续 X 天未登录，再次登录时仍可领取第 2 天奖励。

📆 Weekly Subscription / 周订阅

1. Rewards After Purchase / 购买后奖励

Two types of rewards are available:

有两种类型的奖励：

  • b. Additional Income in 7 Days: Rewards require manual claiming, and the player must log in continuously. Missing a login or failing to claim manually will result in losing the reward. The Day 1 reward can be claimed on the day of purchase.
  • b. 7 天额外收益：奖励需要手动领取，且玩家必须连续登录。错过登录或未能手动领取将导致失去奖励。第 1 天奖励可在购买当天领取。

  • - Multiple Weekly Subscriptions can be purchased. Login rewards are calculated independently.
  • - 可以购买多个周订阅。登录奖励独立计算。
  • - For subscriptions of different prices: Daily login rewards can be claimed separately each day.
  • - 不同价格的订阅：每日登录奖励可以每天分别领取。
  • - For subscriptions of the same price: The daily login reward claim period will be extended.
  • - 相同价格的订阅：每日登录奖励领取期限将延长。

Auto-Renewal Details / 自动续费详情

1. Eligibility & Subscription Tiers / 资格与订阅档位

Currently, select players can sign up for weekly subscription auto-renewal directly on the Weekly Subscription page.

目前，部分玩家可以直接在周订阅页面注册周订阅自动续费。

Auto-Renewal Cycle: Charges occur once per week (weekly billing cycle).

自动续费周期：每周扣费一次（每周计费周期）。

Subscription Tiers: Three options are available: $7, $30, and $90. Players may subscribe to multiple tiers simultaneously (e.g., subscribe to both $7 and $30 tiers at the same time).

订阅档位：有三种选项：$7、$30 和 $90。玩家可以同时订阅多个档位（例如同时订阅 $7 和 $30 档位）。

2. Subscription Process & Reward Distribution / 订阅流程与奖励发放

After selecting the desired auto-renewal tier(s), a payment page will pop up. Players choose their preferred payment method for auto-deduction. Once the first payment is successful, auto-renewal will be activated immediately.

选择所需的自动续费档位后，将弹出支付页面。玩家选择首选支付方式用于自动扣费。首次支付成功后，自动续费将立即激活。

After each successful auto-renewal deduction, players will receive the corresponding subscription rewards via Inbox.

每次自动续费扣费成功后，玩家将通过站内信收到相应的订阅奖励。

3. Notification Mechanisms / 通知机制

Pre-Billing Reminder: Players will receive an email at their registered account email 5 minutes before each auto-renewal deduction. This email includes:

扣费前提醒：玩家将在每次自动续费扣费前 5 分钟收到注册账号邮箱的邮件。该邮件包含：

  • - The date of the upcoming charge
  • - 即将到来的扣费日期
  • - A link to manage their auto-renewal settings
  • - 管理自动续费设置的链接

Failed Subscription Notice: If auto-renewal fails (e.g., insufficient funds, payment method expired), players will receive a "Subscription Failed" email. This email also includes a link to update their purchase method promptly.

订阅失败通知：如果自动续费失败（例如余额不足、支付方式过期），玩家将收到"订阅失败"邮件。该邮件还包含及时更新支付方式的链接。

4. How to Manage Subscriptions / 如何管理订阅

Players can manage their weekly auto-renewal subscriptions (both active and inactive) in-game via the following steps:

玩家可以通过以下步骤在游戏内管理每周自动续费订阅（包括活跃和非活跃的）：

  • 1. Go to the Lobby page
  • 1. 前往大厅页面
  • 2. Click the menu icon in the top-right corner
  • 2. 点击右上角的菜单图标
  • 3. Select "Manage Subscription"
  • 3. 选择"管理订阅"

Within the "Manage Subscription" section, players can:

在"管理订阅"部分，玩家可以：

  • - Update or change their purchase method
  • - 更新或更改支付方式
  • - Cancel active auto-renewal subscriptions
  • - 取消活跃的自动续费订阅
  • - View key details: current auto-renewal status, next billing date, and current payment method
  • - 查看关键详情：当前自动续费状态、下次计费日期和当前支付方式

💰 Cash Back Rewards Back / 返现奖励

Activity Availability / 活动可见性

This activity appears only once and will disappear after the player purchases this item.

此活动仅出现一次，玩家购买后将消失。

Activity Tiers / 活动档位

Randomly available in tiers of $1, $2, and $10.

随机以 $1、$2 和 $10 档位出现。

Rewards After Purchase / 购买后奖励

1. Immediately receive 1 unplayed SC / 2 or 10 unplayed SC.

1. 立即获得 1 个未游玩 SC / 2 或 10 个未游玩 SC。

2. Receive a 100 SC redemption reserve/fund (this 100 SC will not be added to the player's in-game balance).

2. 获得一个 100 SC 提现储备/基金（这 100 SC 不会添加到玩家的游戏余额中）。

Redemption Reserve/Fund Usage Rules / 提现储备/基金使用规则

Players are randomly divided into two groups (do not disclose the grouping to players):

玩家被随机分为两组（请勿向玩家透露分组信息）：

Group A / A组

For each redemption, 10% of the requested redemption amount is deducted from the 100 SC reserve, the remaining 90% is deducted from the player's played SC.

每次提现时，请求提现金额的 10% 从 100 SC 储备中扣除，剩余 90% 从玩家的已游玩 SC 中扣除。

Example: If the player submits a 100 SC redemption, 90 SC is deducted from their balance, and 10 SC is deducted from the reserve.

示例：如果玩家提交 100 SC 提现，90 SC 从其余额中扣除，10 SC 从储备中扣除。

Group B / B组

A 10% rebate is given only for the first redemption and 1% rebate is given for a single redemption of ≥100 SC on the following redemption request.

仅在首次提现时给予 10% 返利，后续单次提现 ≥100 SC 时给予 1% 返利。

Insufficient Reserve Handling / 储备不足处理

Regardless of the group, if 10% of the redemption amount (which should be covered by the reserve) exceeds the remaining reserve balance, only the available reserve balance will be used, and the rest will be deducted from the player's played SC.

无论哪组，如果提现金额的 10%（应由储备覆盖）超过剩余储备余额，仅使用可用储备余额，其余部分从玩家的已游玩 SC 中扣除。

Common Player Misunderstandings / 玩家常见误解

1. Players may mistakenly believe the activity adds an extra 10% to their redemption amount. In reality, the redemption amount remains unchanged—only 10% of it is covered by the reserve.

1. 玩家可能误以为活动在提现金额上额外增加 10%。实际上，提现金额保持不变——只有其中的 10% 由储备覆盖。

2. If players have questions about the redemption amount or suspect the reserve did not cover 10%, check the redemption record in Game Flow. The transaction details will show the exact SC amount deducted from the player's in-game balance. Compare this amount with the total redemption amount to calculate how much SC was covered by the reserve.

2. 如果玩家对提现金额有疑问或怀疑储备未覆盖 10%，请在 Game Flow 中查看提现记录。交易详情将显示从玩家游戏余额中扣除的确切 SC 金额。将此金额与总提现金额对比，即可计算储备覆盖了多少 SC。
